# About

This site collects my thoughts about modeling and simulations, digital twin and industrial AI and the tire industry.

My main areas of interest include:

- AI-native modeling and simulation workflows for product design
- Digital twins for products and processes
- Industrial AI and its path from proof of concept to production
- Manufacturing digitalization in the global and China tire industry

The goal is to explore how these ideas can improve real industrial workflows while keeping engineering judgment, validation, and accountability at the center.
